                        Acceleration is defined as the rate of change in velocity to the change in time. It is denoted by symbol a. a = (v−u)/t

                        The density of material shows the denseness of it in a specific given area.ρ = m/V

                        According to Newton’s second law of motion, the force can be expressed by the product of mass and acceleration of the body.F = m × a

                        The capacity to do some work is termed as Energy. The Energy spent to do work in a unit amount of time is termed as Power.P = W/t
                        6) Weight Formula:
                        Weight is not anything but the force which an object experiences due to gravity.W = mg

                        Kinetic energy is the energy that is possessed by a body due to its state of motion.E = 12mv^2
