1) Average Speed Formula: 
                The average speed is the average of speed of a moving body for the overall distance that it has covered. S = d/t   
                2) Acceleration Formula:
                Acceleration is defined as the rate of change in velocity to the change in time. It is denoted by symbol a. a = (v−u)/t
                3) Density Formula:
                The density of material shows the denseness of it in a specific given area.ρ = m/V
                4) Newton’s Second Law:
                According to Newton’s second law of motion, the force can be expressed by the product of mass and acceleration of the body.F = m × a
                5) Power Formula:
                The capacity to do some work is termed as Energy. The Energy spent to do work in a unit amount of time is termed as Power.P = W/t
                6) Weight Formula:
                Weight is not anything but the force which an object experiences due to gravity.W = mg
                7) Pressure Formula:
                The pressure is defined as the amount of force applied per unit area of the object.P = F/A
                8) Kinetic Energy formula:
                Kinetic energy is the energy that is possessed by a body due to its state of motion.E = 12mv^2 
                9) Ohms Law Formula:
                Ohms law says that the current running through some conductor material is directly proportional to the potential difference between two endpoints of the conductor.V = I × R
                10) Frequency Formula:
                Frequency is the revolutions completed per second or as the number of wave cycles.f = Vλ
